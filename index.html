FAQ

What kind of skills can I learn on DeBug.org?

We offer a wide range of courses covering in-demand skills for the modern workplace. This includes programming languages, web development, data analysis, project management, digital marketing, and more. Browse our course catalog to find what interests you!

Is it really completely free to take courses on DeBug.org?

Yes, all our courses are 100% free to access. We believe everyone should have the opportunity to learn and upskill, regardless of their financial situation. Our operational costs are covered by sponsorships and ad revenue.

How do the courses work? Are they self-paced?

Our courses are primarily self-paced, meaning you can learn at your own speed and convenience. Each course is broken down into modules with video lessons, quizzes, and assignments to help you master the material.

Do I get a certificate upon completing a course?

Yes, upon successfully completing a course and passing any required assessments, you will receive a certificate of completion. This can be a valuable addition to your resume or LinkedIn profile.
